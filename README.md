# India Macro Research — Inflation vs Growth

**"Is Controlling Inflation More Important Than Supporting Growth in India Today?"**  
A 2-page independent macro research report analysing the RBI's monetary policy dilemma

---

## Project Overview

This report examines one of the most debated questions in Indian macroeconomics — whether the Reserve Bank of India should prioritise inflation control over growth support, or vice versa. Using a structured macro framework, the report analyses current economic indicators, RBI policy stance, and makes a clear evidence-based argument.

---

## Research Framework

### Macro Indicators Analysed
- CPI Inflation trajectory and RBI target band (2–6%)
- GDP growth rate and output gap
- Real interest rates and their impact on consumption and investment
- Fiscal deficit and its interaction with monetary policy
- Global factors — Fed rate cycle, commodity prices, INR pressure

### Argument Structure
- Current state of inflation vs growth tradeoff in India
- Historical RBI policy responses in similar cycles
- Risks of prioritising growth prematurely (inflation persistence)
- Risks of over-tightening (growth slowdown, credit contraction)
- Conclusion and policy recommendation
